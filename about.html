About Me

I'm a woman born in 1996 on a small island in Denmark. I live in Wynberg, Cape Town in a townhouse with my partner. I'm an academic particularly interested in the African continent. I've specialized in gender-based violence in South Africa, and hope to combine my passion for social justice with my multimediadesign journey. Read below for more information about me.

Education

  • Currently studying Multimediadesign at The IBA International Business Academy
  • Master of Arts in African Studies from University of Copenhagen. An interdisciplinary program covering politics, religion, development, nature, economics, culture, etc.
  • Bachelor in Global Humanities from Roskilde University. An interdisciplinary program based on four dimensions: science & philosophy, text & sign, subjectivity & learning, history & culture. I did a subject combination of cultural encounters and social psychology.

Personality

  • Central traits: Detail-oriented, perfectionistic, curious, analytical, empathetic, sociable, bubbly
  • 16 Personalities type: Protagonist (ENFJ-T). This result indicates that my personality combines charisma, empathy, and idealism, giving me a natural ability to inspire and lead others. I am highly attuned to people's feelings and motivations, and an effective communicator. Some of my greatest struggles are that my idealistic nature sometimes can lead to disapointment, and my empathy can also be a challenge, as I take on others’ problems as they were my own.

Interests

  • Academic: Social justice, in particular gender-based violence in South Africa. More generally I am interested in anything within humanities and social science, whether it be culture, psychology, language, philosophy.
  • At home: Gardening, cooking, reading, gaming with my partner, crotcheting, writing, painting, watching soccer, DJing
  • Out: Exploring Cape Town, hiking, winefarms, festivals, comedy, concerts (amapiano, 3 step, afrotech, deep house)

Style

  • I like designing things that are fun, vibrant, warm, memorable and not necessarily fitting into the usual minimalistic paradigm
  • I love photography, mostly in nature, and enjoy experimenting with natural frames, textures, colors and lighting.
  • I am a nostalgic at heart, and like to draw inspiration from times that were more expressive and charming.
